# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge / 我们的承诺

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

我们作为成员、贡献者和领导者，承诺使我们的社区成为一个对所有人都没有骚扰的环境，无论年龄、体型、可见或不可见的残疾、种族、性别特征、性别认同和表达、经验水平、教育程度、社会经济地位、国籍、个人外貌、种族、宗教或性取向。

## Our Standards / 我们的标准

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment for our community include:

有助于为我们的社区创造积极环境的行为示例包括：

* Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people / 对他人表现出同理心和善意
* Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences / 尊重不同的意见、观点和经验
* Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback / 给予并优雅地接受建设性反馈
* Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes / 承担责任并向受我们错误影响的人道歉
* Learning from our mistakes / 从错误中学习
* Focusing on what is best not just for us as individuals, but for the overall community / 关注不仅对个人而且对整个社区最有利的事情

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

不可接受的行为示例包括：

* The use of sexualized language or imagery, and sexual attention or advances of any kind / 使用性化语言或图像，以及任何形式的性关注或挑逗
* Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks / 挑衅、侮辱或贬低性评论，以及人身或政治攻击
* Public or private harassment / 公开或私下骚扰
* Publishing others' private information without their permission / 未经许可发布他人的私人信息
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting / 其他在专业环境中可能被合理认为不适当的行为

## Enforcement Responsibilities / 执行责任

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

社区领导者负责澄清和执行我们的可接受行为标准，并将对他们认为不适当、威胁、冒犯或有害的任何行为采取适当和公平的纠正措施。
